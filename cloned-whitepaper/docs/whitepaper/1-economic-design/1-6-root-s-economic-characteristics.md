| --- | --- |
| **Chain Independence** | Operates entirely within TRN without external dependence |

| **Controlled Inflation** | Transition from emission rewards to revenue-backed buybacks |
| **On-Chain Transparency** | All Vault and Treasury activities verifiable on-chain |
| **Governance Connectivity** | Treasury performance feeds directly into DAO policy decisions |
