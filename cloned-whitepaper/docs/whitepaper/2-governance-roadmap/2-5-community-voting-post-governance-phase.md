---
After full DAO deployment:

- Proposal Bond (500–1,000 ROOT) required for submission
- Approved proposals → bond refunded + bonus; rejected → partial burn
- Voters receive pro-rata rewards from DAO Reward Pool

| Role | Incentive |
| --- | --- |
| Voter | Pro-rata DAO Reward Pool distribution |
| Proposal Author | 0.5–1 % ROOT bonus from Treasury |
| Long-term Participant | Tier upgrade → greater weight and reward |
