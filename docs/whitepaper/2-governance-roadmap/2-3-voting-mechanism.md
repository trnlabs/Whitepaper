> **Voting Power = ROOT Balance × vToken Multiplier × Holding Period Factor**

- **ROOT Balance:** baseline voting weight
- **vToken Multiplier:** amplifies weight based on Vault participation
- **Holding Factor:** adds bonus for long-term holding (+20 % example)
